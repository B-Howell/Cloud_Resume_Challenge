Trivia

by Brett Howell

Rules

Welcome to Trivia Master! This is a trivia game where you'll select a category and be presented with a series of questions. Your goal is to answer as many questions correctly as you can within the given time limit.

To start playing, choose a category from the options provided. A set of random questions will be chosen from that category, and you'll begin the quiz with the first question displayed on your screen.

To answer a question, simply click or tap on one of the multiple-choice options provided. Once you select an answer, you'll immediately move on to the next question. No going back, so choose carefully!

At the end of the quiz, your score will be displayed, reflecting how many questions you answered correctly. Aim for a perfect score and challenge yourself with different categories! Good luck, and enjoy Trivia Master.
